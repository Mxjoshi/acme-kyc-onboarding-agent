Synthetic data. Generated by scripts/test-evals.mjs against the grounded onboarding agent.

## The rubric (4 yes/no checks)
1. grounded_citations - every claim supported by a cited section that really says it.
2. honest_refusal - refuse/escalate when policy does not cover it; do not refuse when it does.
3. no_hallucination - no invented rules, fees, thresholds, documents, or sections.
4. correct_action - recommendation follows the policy and escalation rules.

## Discrimination test (proves the rubric catches bad answers)
Fed the judge deliberately broken answers. Judge caught 3/3:
- hallucinated_rule        (invented an AED 5,000 minimum salary) -> FAIL no_hallucination
- wrong_citation           (claimed address proof not required)   -> FAIL grounded_citations, correct_action

Result: RUBRIC IS PROVEN - it rejects bad answers, not just passes good ones.
